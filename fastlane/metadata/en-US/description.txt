VoiceDrop turns what you say into articles.

Open it and it records; tap stop and it's saved. The rest is automatic:

• One-tap recording, keeps going in the background
• Auto-named by time + current location (city / district) so you recognize it later
• Each recording is transcribed to subtitles (SRT) and mined into readable articles
• Listen to the audio, read the subtitles, and read the articles — right in the app
• Minimal interface, no ads, no third-party tracking

Say it, and it's done. VoiceDrop catches every passing thought and writes it up.
